Am I Ready to Vote?

Let's check your documents.

🪪

Do you have your Voter ID (EPIC card)?

✅

You're ready to vote! 🎉

Your EPIC card is the primary document needed at the polling station.

ℹ️

Don't have an EPIC card?

You can still vote with any of these 12 ECI-approved alternate documents:

  • ✅Aadhaar Card
  • ✅Passport
  • ✅Driving License
  • ✅PAN Card
  • ✅MNREGA Job Card
  • ✅Bank Passbook with Photo
  • ✅Health Insurance Smart Card
  • ✅Pension Document with Photo
  • ✅NPR Smart Card
  • ✅Govt Employee ID Card
  • ✅MP/MLA/MLC Identity Card
  • ✅Official Identity Document

Don't have any of these documents? Apply for Voter ID:

Not sure which form?
Call 1950 or visit voters.eci.gov.in

Your Election Day Journey

Follow this timeline to understand exactly what happens at the polling booth.

🌅
7:00 AM

Polling Booth Opens

Booths open at 7 AM. Most close at 5-6 PM. Arrive early to avoid queues.

📋
Arrive

Find Your Name

Officers check the electoral roll. Tell them your name and booth slip number.

Find your name in advance →
🪪
Queue

Identity Check

Show your Voter ID or any of the 12 ECI-approved alternate documents.

🖋️
Inside

Ink on Finger

Indelible ink is applied to your left index finger. This prevents double voting. Fades in 2-3 weeks — completely normal.

🗳️
Vote!

Cast Your Vote on EVM

Find your candidate's name and party symbol. Press the blue button next to it.

← Press here next to your candidate

🔔 Heard a beep? Your vote is cast! ✅

📄
+7 sec

VVPAT Confirms Your Vote

A paper slip appears in the glass window showing your candidate's name, symbol, and party.

🧾

Candidate Name
Party Symbol ✦

Visible for 7 seconds, then drops into sealed box

🚫
Option

NOTA — Your Right to Reject

NOTA stands for None of the Above. It is the last button on every EVM in India.

✅ If no candidate deserves your vote, press NOTA

✅ Your vote still counts as democratic participation

✅ NOTA was introduced by Supreme Court in 2013

✅ It cannot directly cause re-election but sends a strong message

✅
Done!

You've Voted! 🎉

Congratulations! You've fulfilled your democratic duty.
